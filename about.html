About Us

Are you tired of paying for overpriced vehicles? HRS Rentals is an all in one rental platform created for everyone. Launched is 1975 HRS Rentals opened it's first locations in Los Angeles, California. HRS Rentals is 100% owner owned. Our mission is to increase public access to car by making our services highly affordable. Since then HRS rentals has become one of the largest renting companies in the world today. Even today Our mission remains the same mission of providing each customer with the best vehciles.
